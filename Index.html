What is NeuraLark?

NeuraLark is a meme coin concept born from one rule: “Always the same jester bird.” Pepe-style facial structure, duck beak, red/blue jester hat with gold bells, mechanical ear modules, cyber armor with blue LEDs, metallic feather plating, and a subtle AI glow. Baby or adult, serious or unhinged — it’s always the same character.

The Face

A mashup of Pepe’s expression with a duck beak and bird body. Expressive eyes, smug energy, and that “I know something you don’t” CT vibe.

The Armor

Metallic feather plating, cyber chest plate, glowing blue lines and status LEDs. NeuraLark looks like it escaped from an AI lab and wandered into a meme war.

The Hat

Red and blue jester halves with gold bells that feel half carnival, half glitch. You’ll see it in every meme — the instant visual fingerprint of $LARK.
